The ABACUS Foods' Story

On the continent of Africa, across countries, cultures and traditions, there has always been a common belief that food is medicine!. At ABACUS Foods, we are committed to preserving and celebrating our rich culinary and wellness heritage. We use 100% natural ingredients grown by farmers in rich, fertile soil, seasonally, hand-harvested and sun-dried. Our dedicated team cleans, blends and packs the spices and seasoning with care. From the farm to your plate, we improve the nutrition, taste and lives of everyone who grows, blends and enjoys the ABACUS experience.
